Data Deletion Instructions

If you have logged in with Facebook and want your data deleted from our systems, you can do so using one of the following methods:

Method 1: Remove App from Facebook

Follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to Settings > Apps and Websites.
  3. Find CBDC Research App in the list.
  4. Click Remove.

Once removed, all data shared with our app will be permanently deleted.

Method 2: Email Request

You may also request data deletion by emailing us at arsalanshaikh0408@gmail.com. Please include the email address linked to your Facebook account. We will delete your data within 24 hours of receiving the request.
